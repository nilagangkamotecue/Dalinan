About Dalinan

Dalinan was founded in 2020 by a group of environmentalists and laundry industry professionals who saw the massive plastic waste problem in the laundry sector.

We developed a revolutionary paper-based packaging system that maintains the detergent's effectiveness while eliminating plastic waste. Our formula is biodegradable and safe for all water systems.

Today, Dalinan is trusted by over 500 laundry shops nationwide, helping them reduce their environmental impact while delivering superior cleaning results.
